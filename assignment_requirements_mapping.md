# Assignment Requirements Mapping
## Management in Digital Economy Assignment

### Assignment Activity Requirements

#### 1. Compare at least three digital-led management approaches (systems, contingency, behavioural, decision-theory)
✅ **COMPLETED IN**: Presentation Slides 3-5
- Systems Approach: Integrated digital processes and workflows
- Contingency Approach: Situational adaptation using real-time data
- Behavioural Approach: People-centered practices in digital environments
- Decision-Theory Approach: Data-driven decision making with analytics tools

#### 2. Analyse digital leadership styles (transformational, participative, visionary) and capabilities (digital champions, pioneers, transformers)
✅ **COMPLETED IN**: Presentation Slides 6-7
- Transformational Leadership: Inspiring innovation and change
- Participative Leadership: Collaborative decision-making in digital teams
- Visionary Leadership: Future-focused strategy in digital contexts
- Digital Capabilities: Champions, Pioneers, Transformers roles and characteristics

#### 3. Apply motivational theories (Maslow, Herzberg, Vroom) to propose strategies to enhance team performance
✅ **COMPLETED IN**: Presentation Slides 8-11
- Maslow's Hierarchy: Adapted for digital workplace needs
- Herzberg's Two-Factor Theory: Hygiene factors and motivators in digital teams
- Vroom's Expectancy Theory: Performance-reward linkage in measurable environments
- Practical strategies for implementation

#### 4. Integrate reflective models (Gibbs, Schön) to demonstrate how leaders can self-evaluate and improve
✅ **COMPLETED IN**: Presentation Slides 12-13
- Gibbs' Reflective Cycle: Structured approach to learning from experiences
- Schön's Reflective Practice: Reflection-in-action and reflection-on-action
- Digital applications: Feedback systems and learning analytics

#### 5. Provide practical, evidence-based recommendations for digital team leadership
✅ **COMPLETED IN**: Presentation Slides 14-15
- Hybrid management approach combining systems and behavioral focus
- Integrated leadership development combining multiple styles
- Balanced motivation strategy with intrinsic and extrinsic elements
- Regular reflection practices using digital tools
